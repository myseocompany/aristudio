## Naming and Planning Rules for the LLM

When proposing July tasks, follow these rules:

- Prefer short, operational task names.
- Avoid names based on attendees, such as `reunión con X y Y`, unless attendance itself is the work.
- Prefer names based on outcome or function:
  - `Revisión SEO web`
  - `Optimizar campaña Meta`
  - `Revisar leads calificados`
  - `Redistribuir leads`
- Avoid underscores and mixed technical formatting in task names.
- If two tasks differ only by person, rewrite them by workstream, not by owner name.
- Reuse the established MQE style: campaign, review, optimize, create, publish, call, audit, import, analyze.

## Constraints for the Planner

- Assume today is 2026-06-22.
- Plan only the remaining days of June and all of July.
- Do not propose April historical tasks as if they were new work.
- Do not over-index on repost noise unless there is a clear campaign reason.
- Prioritize work that converts existing demand before proposing net-new side projects.
